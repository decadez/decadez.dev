This site is a small place for durable notes.

The plan is simple: keep the site easy to edit, keep posts close to the code,
and publish the pieces that are useful enough to revisit.

## What belongs here

- Engineering notes that explain a decision or pattern.
- AI workflow experiments that can be repeated.
- Product observations that are sharper after being written down.
- Small project logs that help future work start faster.

## What does not belong here

This is not a heavy portfolio or a polished marketing site. It is a working
notebook with enough structure to stay useful.
